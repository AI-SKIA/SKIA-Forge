# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and harassment-free environment for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in communication and review feedback.
- Focus on technical outcomes, not personal attacks.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Threats, intimidation, or doxxing.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration workflows.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to: `conduct@skia.local`.
